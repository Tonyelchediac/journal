Assia: Heaven on Earth. A Hidden Gem of Heritage, Tourism and Pottery

Assia, a tranquil village in high mountains Batroun District, Lebanon. Is famous for its ancient pottery craft, Roman-era ruins, and scenic hiking trails. Renowned for handmade red clay pottery passed down by women for generations, the village also features historic churches, natural springs, and oak forests. One of its most breathtaking spots is the Lady of the Fortress waterfall, a peaceful haven surrounded by greenery, perfect for nature lovers.
