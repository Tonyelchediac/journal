Batroun: The Capital of Tourism in Arab World

Batroun, a picturesque coastal city in northern Lebanon, has earned its reputation as the country's capital of tourism. Known for its stunning Mediterranean beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich historical heritage, Batroun attracts visitors from all over the world. The city boasts ancient Phoenician walls, charming old souks, and a lively waterfront lined with cafes and restaurants. Its annual festivals, fresh seafood, and welcoming atmosphere make Batroun a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a blend of relaxation, culture, and adventure in Lebanon.
